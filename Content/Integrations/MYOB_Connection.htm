Connecting to MYOB

Follow the steps listed here to set up MYOB integration with Tradiecore. Tradiecore supports full synchronisation with MYOB. Any changes you make to an invoice or customer in MYOB is automatically updated in Tradiecore and any changes made in Tradiecore will be reflected in MYOB.

Note:

In order to connect to MYOB, you will require a subscription. For information on how to set up a Xero account see, https://www.myob.com/au/support

  1. On the Home screen, select Settings → Integrations.

    The Integrations screen will be displayed.

  2. In the Available Connections section, select Connect to MYOB.

    This will open the MYOB login screen.

  3. Enter the email address of your MYOB account and select Next.

  4. Enter the password of your MYOB account and select Sign in.

  5. For verification, you will be prompted to enter a code sent to you by MYOB. Enter the code and select Verify.

    MYOB then displays a screen with all the company files you have.

  6. Using the drop-down list, choose the company file that you want to connect with Tradiecore and select Continue.

  7. Your customer and invoice information will be automatically synchronised between MYOB and Tradiecore. This process may take up-to 10 minutes. When complete, select Ok, lets go.

  8. Next, set up your chart of accounts, by selecting the General option. ﻿

    This displays a list of all the accounts you have in MYOB.

  9. Choose the account you want, and select Update.

    When MYOB has successfully connected to Tradiecore the Integrations screen is updated to show your account connection. Your invoices will also be updated to show that they have been sent to MYOB.
