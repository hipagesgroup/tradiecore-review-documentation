Invoices

The Invoices screen and the Invoices section within a job are your main workspaces for creating, editing, and managing invoices. You can create multiple invoices for a job if needed, for example, to issue progress payments.

The Invoices list gives you an overview of all invoices you’ve created in hipages for business. From here, you can add, edit, or delete draft invoices, and use the filter or search tools to quickly find what you’re looking for.

Invoices help you keep your payments organised and make it easy to track every job from quote to completion. Each invoice is colour-coded and clearly labelled to show its current status:

  • Draft - The invoice has been created but not sent

  • Sent - The invoice has been sent to your customer.

  • Paid - Payment has been received.

  • Cancelled - The invoice is no longer active.

Note:

Every invoice in hipages for business must be linked to a job. You can’t create an invoice without a corresponding job.

Watch the Creating Invoices video below, or see Creating a new invoice for more information on creating an invoice in hipages for business.

If you’ve set up hipages Payments, your customer will receive a secure payment link in their invoice email to pay by card. The invoice PDF also includes your bank details (BSB and account number) for customers who prefer to pay via bank transfer.

When a customer pays using digital payments, the invoice status automatically updates to paid.

If the customer pays by bank transfer, the status won’t update automatically, in that case, simply mark the invoice as paid manually once the payment is confirmed.
