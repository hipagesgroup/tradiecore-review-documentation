Enabling appointment notifications and reminders

hipages for business, gives you control over how you and your customers stay informed about upcoming appointments.

Three types of notifications can be sent:

  • Customer booking notifications – sent immediately when you schedule an appointment

  • Customer reminder notifications – sent the day before the appointment

  • Self reminders – push notifications sent to you before the appointment starts

These options help keep everyone on track and reduce the chance of missed appointments.

Notifying your customers

When you create an appointment, you can choose to notify your customer straight away. This sends them an SMS with the appointment details.

To send a message to your customer,

  • Select Notify Customer, in the pop up message that is shown when you save an appointment.

  • When the message has been successfully sent, a confirmation message will be shown. Select ok to close it.

Reminding your customers

You can also send your customers an automatic reminder one day before the appointment. The reminder is sent by SMS at 10:00 am and includes the appointment details.

This helps reduce no-shows and keeps your customers informed without extra effort on your part.

A reminder won’t be sent if the appointment was created the day before it’s scheduled (to prevent double notifications).

To enable the option,

  • On the appointment screen, turn the Send customer reminders option on.

    When enabled, an SMS reminder is automatically sent to the customer the day before the appointment. A copy of the reminder is also saved in the Photos & Notes section of the job.

Note:

To send SMS reminders, a valid customer phone number must be saved in their profile. If no phone number is available, the Send customer reminders option will be unavailable (greyed out).

Enabling reminders for yourself

The Notify me option lets you set personal reminders so you never miss an appointment. By default, you’ll receive a push notification 30 minutes before the appointment starts.

To adjust your reminder time,

  • Select Notify me.

  • Choose your preferred reminder time from the list.
