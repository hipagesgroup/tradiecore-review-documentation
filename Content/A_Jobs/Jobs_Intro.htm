Jobs

The Jobs tab is where you manage all your work in hipages, from quoting and invoicing through to scheduling and job updates. When you accept a lead, a job is created automatically so you can get straight to it.

Note:

Automatic job creation requires an active subscription. You can also manually create jobs for work that comes from outside hipages.

Your jobs list

All your jobs appear in a list when you tap the Jobs tab. At a glance, you can see what's on the go and what's already been wrapped up. Use filters to narrow your list by job status, and sorting to control the order jobs appear in.

Each job shows the most recent activity, including whether an estimate, quote, or invoice has been sent and its status, so you can see at a glance if a quote's been approved or is still pending. If no action has been taken yet, you'll see a prompt to create an estimate to get the ball rolling.

Tap any job to open it.

Job Overview

The Job Overview is your home base for each job. Here's what you'll find:

  • Customer details and job description

  • Quick-create icons to kick off an estimate, quote, invoice, or appointment

  • The Job Diary, where you can track notes and a full history of job activity

Quotes, invoices, and appointments

These now live in their own dedicated tabs at the top of the Job Overview screen, keeping things a bit more manageable as a job grows.

Tap the relevant tab to see everything related to that part of the job:

  • Quotes — view and manage all quotes sent to your customer

  • Invoices — track and send invoices for the job

  • Schedule — see all scheduled appointments linked to the job
