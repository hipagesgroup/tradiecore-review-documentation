Walkthrough of the setup process

When you first log in to hipages tradiecore, you’ll go through a short welcome process along with several milestones. It’s designed to help you set up your profile, learn the basics, and start using the app with confidence.

Milestone List

Milestones are key set up steps that help complete your account. Each one you finish unlocks a free lead to help you start winning jobs straight away.

Your milestones are:

  • Adding a profile image

  • Adding 5 photos of the work you've done

  • Accepting your first 5 leads

  • Contacting 10 new customers

  • Sending your first quote

The welcome process includes:

  1. A quick Profile set up.

    This is the first thing customers see so take a moment to add your business details, a profile photo, and examples of your past work.

  2. Video on how hipages works.

    It gives you a quick look at how the app works and how to make the most of your leads.

  3. Early milestones of uploading a profile image and photos of your work.

    Upload a profile photo, add job photos, and complete a few key steps. Every time you hit a milestone, you’ll earn a free lead.

    Quick tip!

    Each time you complete a milestone you'll get 1 free lead.

  4. If you skip a milestone during this stage or want to check your progress, you can view and complete them any time from the To Do section.

Once you're done, you’re ready to start winning work, quoting, and managing your business more efficiently.
