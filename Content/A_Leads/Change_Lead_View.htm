Changing your lead view

The Lead view feature lets you choose how you’d like to see your leads in the app. You can switch between a compact list, a fully expanded card view, or a map view, depending on whether you want speed, detail, or a visual overview. By default, leads appear in Compact cards.

Compact cards show the key details at a glance, including job type, location, lead value, start requirement, customer name, posting date, job description and any available images.

To change your lead view: 

  1. On the Leads screen, select the view icon .

  2. From the View leads as menu choose one of these options:

    • Compact cards - view leads in a collapsed view. This view will let you see more leads without having to scroll.

    • Expanded cards - view all the details related to a job.

    • Map cards - view the leads on a map based on the location of your business. The leads displayed on the map view are dependant on the areas that you have specified in Lead Settings.

Using the map view

Map view gives you a visual overview of how many leads are available in each area. Each suburb is marked with a number showing the total leads you can review.

  1. To view the leads for an area, select the number. A panel will appear at the bottom of the screen showing the available leads and how many you’re currently viewing.

    Note:

    The map shows the total number of leads per suburb, not the exact location of individual leads.

  2. Swipe left or right to move through the lead cards.

    The counter at the top updates as you scroll, so you always know where you’re up to.

  3. If you want to see more detail, select the expand arrow to open the full lead card. From here you can:

    • Accept the lead

    • Decline the lead

  4. To close the panel, select the close icon .

  5. To switch back to a list view at any time, close the lead panel and select List view.
