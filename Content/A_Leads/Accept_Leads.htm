Accepting leads

To accept hipages leads in the hipages for business app you must have a hipages subscription with sufficient credit. If you have run out of credit, you will not be able to accept leads in hipages for business. Please contact hipages for further assistance.

Finding the right leads

The Leads screen has three tabs to help you browse and prioritise your leads:

  • All — shows all your available leads in one view

  • Premium leads — highlights leads that include signals of high-value or high-intent work. Use this tab if you want to focus on leads that may have a stronger chance of converting

  • Today's deals — showcases unclaimed leads as special offers at a reduced credit rate, available for a limited time each day

Premium leads are easy to spot in the list, each one displays a banner showing what makes it a premium opportunity, such as "Renovation project" or "Installation required."

To accept leads,

  1. Select the Leads tab in the navigation bar.

  2. Select the tab that best suits what you're looking for — All, Premium leads, or Today's deals — then scroll through the list to browse available leads.

    You can also switch to map view to see leads based on your location. For more information, see Changing your lead view.

  3. Slide down the screen to view more information about the job.

  4. To view the photos of the job attached to the lead, select an image.

  5. To accept the lead, select Accept, then confirm by selecting Accept again when prompted

    Once accepted, you'll have two options for what to do next.

Joining the waitlist

If a lead has already been accepted by three other businesses, you'll see Join waitlist on the lead card instead of Accept. For more information on joining the waitlist and how credits are charged, see Waitlisting leads.

Send an SMS estimate

Enter a cost estimate for the job and select Preview SMS to open the message in your SMS app, ready to send to your customer. For more information, see Sending a job estimate.

View the job

Select View Job to open the job details, where you can create and send quotes, set up appointments, and manage other job-related activity.
