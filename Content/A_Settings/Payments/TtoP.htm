Tap to Pay

Tap to Pay in hipages tradiecore lets you accept payments from your customers instantly right from your mobile device. Customers can pay by tapping their card or mobile wallet on your phone, and the payment is processed on the spot.

It’s a simple, secure, and contactless way to get paid that helps reduce admin time and improves your cash flow. With Tap to Pay, there’s no need to wait for digital payment links or bank transfers, you can take care of payments before leaving the job site.

Enabling Tap to Pay

  1. You can enabl Tap to Pay in any of the following methods:

    • From the To Do screen, select the Tap to Pay message.

      iOS

      Android

    • From an invoice, go to the Digital Payments section and select Enable Tap to Pay.

      iOS

      Android

    • Go to Profile → My Business → Quotes & Invoices Settings, and select the Tap to Pay option.

      iOS

      Android

  2. Then, complete the setup based on your device:

    • On iPhone, select Enable Tap to Pay on iPhone.

    • On Android, select Initialise Payment Provider.

    This enables the payment feature on your device.

    iOS

    Android

    Note:

    On Android devices, you’ll be prompted to grant location permissions, including access to your device’s location and nearby devices. Select Allow for both prompts to continue.

Accepting Payment

Once Tap to Pay is enabled,

  1. Open the invoice and select Tap to Pay under Payments methods.

  2. Hold out your device for your customer to tap their card or mobile wallet.

    When the payment is successfully processed, a confirmation message will appear and you’ll be taken to the Job Details screen.
