Quotes and Invoices Settings

The Quotes and Invoices Settings screen is where you can manage how your business handles quotes, invoices, and payments.

From here, you can set up your payment terms, choose your payment methods, update your business address, and add your terms and conditions for quotes and invoices.

hipages tradiecore supports two ways for you to receive payments from customers:

Digital Payments

Digital Payments in hipages for business are powered by hipages payments, a fast and secure way to get paid when you send quotes or invoices.

With hipages Payments, you can:

  • Accept major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX)

  • Accept digital wallet payments including Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Tap to Pay

  • Automatically include a secure payment link in your customer’s invoice email

Payments are processed securely through Stripe. Funds are deposited to your nominated bank account within two business days after your customer makes a payment. For hipages payments set up instructions, see Connecting to hipages Payments.

Bank Transfers

If you don't have digital payments set up, you can receive payments directly to your bank account via bank transfer.

Simply add your BSB and account number in hipages tradiecore. These details are automatically included in the PDF quotes and invoices you send to customers.

For information on how to set your banking details, see Setting up bank details and transfers.
