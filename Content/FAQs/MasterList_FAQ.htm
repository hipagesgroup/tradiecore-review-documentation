Full Tradiecore FAQs

Browse the sections below to find answers to our most commonly asked questions about Tradiecore.

About Us
Who is Tradiecore?
Tradiecore is part of the hipages Group and we are on a mission to enable small trade businesses to thrive.
What is Tradiecore?
Tradiecore is a job management app, built exclusively for small trade businesses in Australia. It lets you manage your jobs, create quotes and invoices, as well as manage your customers on the go.
What is job management and job admin?
'Job management' and 'job admin' refers to all the paperwork or administrative tasks that tradies need to do to manage a job; from when they first receive an enquiry from a potential customer, all the way through to getting paid for the completed work. This includes quoting, invoicing, scheduling, communicating with customers and taking payments.
How will Tradiecore help my business become more efficient?
Using Tradiecore's technology can help you save time. Including our app to create and send quotes and invoices can mean you have more time to spend on the tools, and avoid working overtime doing admin.
What features can I expect next from Tradiecore?
We're constantly building new features that are designed to increase your productivity and give you more insights into your business. More powerful integrations that allow you to drive business growth and profitability, such as scheduling jobs, will be available soon.
Is Tradiecore available on iOS and Android?
Yes, Tradiecore is available on iOS devices and Android devices. It can be downloaded from the App Store or Google Play Store.
Is Tradiecore available on Desktop?
Not yet. We will continue to keep all customers up to date as we progress with feature and platform releases.
Why do I need to add my ABN and Business address?

This is important information for your customers and will be included on all the invoices you produce through Tradiecore. You can also add your ABN to Quotes, if you choose. You can add or update these details any time in the app under Settings.

How do I cancel my account?
You can send your cancellation request through to our team via support@tradiecore.com.au We're constantly learning and would love to know more about why you are cancelling so please include as much information and feedback as possible.
Getting Started
Why has my navigation bar changed?

Your navigation bar has changed because we have updated the app and the navigation bar to let you accept hipages leads in real time in Tradiecore.

The navigation bar in Tradiecore has been updated to include the Leads icon. Select Leads to access the Leads screen which displays all the leads applicable to you, in Tradiecore.

Note:

You must have a hipages subscription in order to view and receive hipages leads in Tradiecore.

Why don’t I have leads?

If your navigation bar includes a Leads tab, but you cannot see any leads, you will need to subscribe to hipages and set up a hipages account.

How do I see my quote and invoice lists?

Your Invoices and Quotes lists are now accessed from the Jobs screen. Select Jobs , the Iinvoices and quotes options are displayed at the top of the screen.

hipages Leads
What are hipages leads?

hipages leads are jobs posted by customers looking to hire a tradie. A lead could be posted by individual customers directly on the hipages app or they could be posted by hipages partners such as IKEA or the Department of Education.

Leads that you see in Tradiecore are the same leads that you receive via the hipages app. For convenience you can now manage your hipages leads from Tradiecore. When you accept a lead in Tradiecore, a corresponding job is automatically created in the Jobs section in Tradiecore. All your admin related to the job can then be managed by opening the job.

What do you need to set up to receive and accept leads?

In order to receive and accept leads, you will need a paid hipages subscription that is connected to Tradiecore via the Integrations option in Settings.

How long does it take for leads to appear in Tradiecore?

Leads are displayed in Tradiecore concurrent with hipages in order to ensure seamless experience across both products.

How do I accept leads?

To find out how leads are accepted in Tradiecore, see Accepting leads in Tradiecore.
