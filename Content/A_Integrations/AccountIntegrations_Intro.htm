Connected Apps

Connected Apps is where you set up connections between hipages for business and your other business tools, including hipages Payments and your accounting software.

In the hipages for business app, tap your Profile , then select Settings & Support → Connected Apps.

Connecting hipages for business to your other tools means less time switching between apps and more time focused on your jobs. Here's what you'll get:

  • Customer information and invoice data sync to your accounting software automatically, keeping your records accurate and up to date

  • Invoicing is streamlined across your tools, so you're not entering the same information twice

  • With accurate, up-to-date financials, you can manage cash flow and make better business decisions

  • Accept payments from customers quickly and securely through hipages Payments

hipages for business is integrated with Xero, QuickBooks, MYOB, and hipages Payments. Select a tile below to get started.

How accounting sync works

Before connecting your accounting software, it's worth understanding how syncing works between hipages for Business and your accounting platform.

First-time connection

A full sync only happens the first time you connect your accounting software to hipages for Business. This means all your existing data is brought across on initial setup.

Disconnecting and reconnecting

If you disconnect and reconnect your accounting software, only invoices created after the reconnection will sync across. Previously synced invoices won't be affected.

Switching accounting platforms

If you switch to a different accounting platform, only invoices created after the new connection will sync. Any invoices previously synced to your old platform will remain there and won't transfer across. We recommend exporting your data from your previous platform and importing it into your new one before connecting hipages for Business.

Note:

Invoices that were synced to your previous platform will continue to show that platform's logo in hipages for Business. This is a known limitation and won't affect your current connection.

Invoice numbers

Invoice numbers in hipages for Business may not match the invoice numbers in your accounting software. This usually happens when your accounting software was already in use before connecting to hipages for Business. In this case, the invoice will be assigned the next available number in your accounting software.

Invoices created in your accounting software

Only invoices created in hipages for Business will sync to your accounting software. Invoices created directly in your accounting software won't be pushed into hipages for Business.
