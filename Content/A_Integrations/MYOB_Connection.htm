Connecting to MYOB

Connect your MYOB account to hipages for Business to keep your invoices and customer information in sync across both apps. Any changes made in either app will automatically update in the other.

Before you start

You'll need an active MYOB subscription to connect. If you don't have one yet, visit MYOB's support page to get set up before continuing.

Steps

  1. In the hipages for business app, go to Profile → Settings & Support → Connected Apps

    The Connected Apps screen is displayed.

  2. Under Available Connections, tap Connect to MYOB.

    The MYOB login screen will open.

  3. Enter the email address of your MYOB account and select Next.

  4. Enter the password of your MYOB account and select Sign in.

  5. Enter the verification code sent to you by MYOB and tap Verify code.

  6. From the drop-down list, select the company file you want to connect to hipages for business, then tap Continue.

  7. Your customer and invoice information will begin syncing between MYOB and hipages for businesss. This process may take up to 10 minutes. When complete, select Ok, lets go.

Setting up or changing your chart of accounts

Once you've successfully connected your accounting software, set up your chart of accounts to choose which revenue account your financial transactions are recorded against.

  1. Under Connected Accounts, tap the row for your accounting software.

  2. On the Preferences screen, tap General under Revenue Accounts.

  3. From the list of revenue accounts set up in your accounting software, select the account you want to record financial transactions against.

  4. Tap Update in the upper right corner to save your selection.

For more information on the chart of accounts see, Chart of Accounts.

What gets synced between MYOB and hipages for business

Once connected, here's what syncs between the two apps:

  • MYOB to hipages for business: your MYOB contacts are synced into hipages for business

  • hipages for business to MYOB: new customers created in the app who have been sent an invoice, along with the invoice itself

Note:

New customers created in hipages for business who haven't been sent an invoice won't be synced to MYOB.
