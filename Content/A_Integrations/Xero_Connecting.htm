Connecting to Xero

Connect your Xero account to hipages for Business to keep your invoices and accounting data in sync across both apps. Once connected, any changes you make to an invoice in either app will automatically sync across both.

Before you start

You'll need an active Xero subscription to connect. If you don't have one yet, create a Xero account before continuing. For information on how to set up a Xero account see, Create a Xero Account.

Note:

Invoices only sync to Xero once they've been sent to your customer by email. Invoices in Draft won't be updated.

Steps

  1. Select Profile → Settings & Support → Connected Apps.

  2. Under Available Connections, select Connect to Xero.

    The Xero login screen will open.

  3. Enter your Xero email address and password, then tap Login.

    Note:

    If you have MFA enabled on your Xero account, see Logging in with Xero MFA enabled before tapping Login.

  4. On the Xero confirmation screen, tap Allow Access to give hipages for business permission to send invoice and accounting data to your Xero account.

    This is to confirm whether hipages for business can have access to your Xero account. hipages for business requires access so we can send invoice and accounting information to your Xero account automatically.

  5. Next, select Ok, lets go to begin setting up your accounting preferences.

Setting up or changing your chart of accounts

Once you've successfully connected your accounting software, set up your chart of accounts to choose which revenue account your financial transactions are recorded against.

  1. Under Connected Accounts, tap the row for your accounting software.

  2. On the Preferences screen, tap General under Revenue Accounts.

  3. From the list of revenue accounts set up in your accounting software, select the account you want to record financial transactions against.

  4. Tap Update in the upper right corner to save your selection.

For more information on the chart of accounts see, Chart of Accounts.

What gets synced between Xero and hipages for business

Once connected, here's what syncs between the two apps:

  • Xero to hipages for business: your Xero contacts are synced into hipages for business

  • hipages for business to Xero: new customers created in the app who have been sent an invoice, along with the invoice itself

Note:

New customers created in hipages for business who haven't been sent an invoice won't be synced to Xero.
