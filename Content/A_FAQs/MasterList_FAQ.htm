hipages for business FAQs

Browse the sections below to find answers to our most commonly asked questions about hipages for business.

About us
Who is hipages

hipages is a platform that connects homeowners with trusted local tradies to make home improvement easier. We provide a two-way solution. Homeowners post jobs and are matched with suitable tradies in their area while tradies receive quality job leads which can become winning jobs. Whether you're a sole trader or a medium-sized trade business, hipages can help increase your revenue so you can grow your business.

What is hipages for business?

hipages for business is an app for Australian tradies to manage their work in one place. It lets you receive and respond to leads, manage jobs, send quotes and invoices, and keep on top of your schedule while you’re on the move.

What is job management and job admin?

'Job management' and 'job admin' refers to all the paperwork or administrative tasks that tradies need to do to manage a job; from when they first receive an enquiry from a potential customer, all the way through to getting paid for the completed work. This includes quoting, invoicing, scheduling, communicating with customers and taking payments.

How will hipages for business help my business become more efficient?

Using hipages for business's technology can help you save time. Including our app to create and send quotes and invoices can mean you have more time to spend on the tools, and avoid working overtime doing admin.

What features can I expect next from hipages for business?

We're constantly building new features that are designed to increase your productivity and give you more insights into your business. More powerful integrations that allow you to drive business growth and profitability, such as scheduling jobs, will be available soon.

Is hipages for business available on iOS and Android?

Yes, hipages for business is available on iOS devices and Android devices. It can be downloaded from the App Store or Google Play Store.

Why do I need to add my ABN and Business address?

This is important information for your customers and will be included on all the invoices you produce through hipages for business. You can also add your ABN to Quotes, if you choose. You can add or update these details any time in the app under Profile Menu.

How do I cancel my account?

You can send your cancellation request by filling in the hipages contact form. We're constantly learning and would love to know more about why you are cancelling so please include as much information and feedback as possible.

hipages leads
What are hipages leads?

hipages leads are jobs posted by customers looking to hire a tradie. A lead could be posted by individual customers directly on the hipages app or they could be posted by hipages partners such as IKEA or Bunnings.

Leads that you see in hipages for business are the same leads that you receive via the hipages app. For convenience you can now manage your hipages leads from hipages for business. When you accept a lead in hipages for business, a corresponding job is automatically created in the Jobs section in hipages for business. All your admin related to the job can then be managed by opening the job.

What do you need to set up to receive and accept leads?

In order to receive and accept leads, you will need a paid hipages subscription that is connected to hipages for business via the Connected Apps option in Profile Menu.

If you do not have a hipages subscription, the screen will be blank and you will need to sign up for a subscription.

What are credits?

Credits are included in your hipages subscription and are used to accept leads. Each subscription package comes with a set amount of lead credit, based on the package you’re on.

If you run out of credits, you can add more by purchasing a lead credit top-up. Any credits you have must be used within the time period set out in your subscription or lead credit pack.

If you’re unsure how much credit is right for your business, hipages support can help you choose a package that suits your needs.

How do I use my lead credit?

You can use your credits to accept job leads. Based on your selected profile settings, such as trade category and location, we will provide you with relevant job leads.

No more than three tradies can accept a job lead, unless both the homeowner and tradie agree on waitlisting.

What is a genuine lead?

Despite our best efforts, we do sometimes get duplicate leads or ones with incorrect contact details. You’ll never need to pay for these, or ones that come from outside your area. If you come across any of these, just let us know and we’ll get it fixed.

In the hipages for business app you can see all of your accepted leads in the Jobs tab. If the job is a duplicate job or has incorrect information contact support using the Message Centre.

Make sure to do this within seven days of accepting the lead, and we’ll take care of the rest. Any leads that fall outside of these situations are genuine leads. Still have questions? Reach out to our support team.

What is map view?

When can I report a lead?

You can report a lead any time within 7 days of accepting it. We will then review your lead report within 5 business days and inform you of the outcome.

How do I report a lead?

You can report a lead by selecting the report icon in the Customer Enquiry section. For more information see Reporting a lead in-app.

Why can’t I report a lead as wrong location or category?

This is because on two previous instances you have reported leads using these options. If you still need to report the lead, choose another applicable category.

If you do not want to receive leads in areas or job categories that you do not service, please update your Lead Settings. For information on how to update your location preferences see Changing coverage areas and to update your category preferences see Changing category preferences.

What is the waitlist?

The waitlist is a way to express your interest in a job that's already been accepted by three other businesses. When you join the waitlist, the customer receives your business details and can choose to request a quote from you on top of the businesses they already have lined up.

Do I have to join the waitlist?

No, joining the waitlist is completely optional.

Do I get charged credits for joining the waitlist?

Joining the waitlist is free. You're only charged credits if the customer releases you from the waitlist and connects with you within 48 hours.

What happens when a customer accepts your business from the waitlist?

When a customer accepts your business from the waitlist, the lead is converted to a job and will appear in your Jobs tab in the hipages for business app. You'll also receive an email notification letting you know.

If the customer accepts your business within 48 hours of you joining the waitlist, the lead credit amount for the lead will be charged. If it's after 48 hours, no credits are charged.

Jobs
Why is my customer still receiving payment reminders after they’ve paid?

Automatic payment reminders are linked to the job status.

If the job is not marked as Done, reminders may continue, even if payment has already been received.

To stop reminders, open the job and update the status to Done once the job is fully completed and paid.

If the job remains in Work Completed or Invoiced, the system assumes payment is still outstanding and may continue sending reminders.

GST

What does tax inclusion/exclusion mean?

The tax inclusion/exclusion options are used to specify if the values that you have entered in your quote or invoice include the GST tax amounts or not. With both options, hipages for business will automatically calculate the applicable GST amounts and update the quote or invoice.

  • Tax Inclusive - Indicates that the invoice amount that you are entering includes the GST tax amount. When you select this option, hipages for business will not add the tax amount to the subtotal. Instead the GST row will calculate the tax amount based on the value you entered and only display it.

  • Tax Exclusive - Indicates that the invoice values that you are entering do not include the GST tax amounts. In this case, hipages for business will calculate the tax amount for each of the items that you enter and add the tax amount to the final total of the invoice.

By default hipages for business applies the Tax Exclusive option to all invoices.

When should I use Tax Inclusive or Tax Exclusive?

It depends on whether the prices you're entering already include GST or not.

Tax Inclusive — choose this if your prices already include GST. hipages for Business will extract and display the GST component, but won't add it again to the total.

For example, if you enter $50 for a line item::

You select the Tax Inclusive option.

  • Subtotal: $50.00

  • GST: $4.55

  • Total: $50.00

Tax Exclusive — choose this if your prices don't include GST. hipages for Business will automatically calculate GST and add it to your total.

For example, if you enter $50 for a line item:

  • Subtotal: $50.00

  • GST: $5.00

  • Total: $55.00

Note:

Tax Exclusive is the default setting for all new quotes and invoices.

Can I remove GST from a quote or invoice?

Yes, but it needs to be done line item by line item. There's no option to remove GST across an entire quote or invoice in one go.

To remove GST from a line item:

  1. Open your quote or invoice.

  2. Select the line item you want to update.

  3. Change the tax rate from GST 10% to None.

  4. Repeat for each line item you want to remove GST from.

Your total will update automatically as you make changes.

Invoices
When are Invoice reminders sent?

Invoice payment reminders are sent automatically to your customers, 3 days before the due date and 7 days after. If you mark an invoice as paid, reminders will not be sent to your customers. So remember to mark your invoices as paid, to avoid unnecessary reminders being sent out to customers.

How can I turn on Invoice reminders?

Invoice payment reminders are turned On by default for each invoice. You can see the reminder status and change at any time from the Automatic Reminders option of each invoice.

How can I disable Reminders?

To disable invoice reminders, see Disabling Reminders.

If my customer has made a deposit payment is it included in the final invoice?

Yes, if your customer has made a deposit payment that for a quote, the deposit amount will be deducted from the final invoice.

What is Tap to Pay?

Tap to Pay is a new feature that allows you to accept payments instantly from your customers. Currently, this feature is only available on Apple devices. Instead of waiting for payments through a digital link, Tap to Pay lets you process transactions immediately, as long as you’ve connected withhipages payments and turned on the Tap to Pay feature on hipages for business. This method offers a faster, more secure, and simpler way to accept payments.

You can accept payments from any contactless card, Apple Pay, or other digital wallets. To troubleshoot Tap to Pay on your Apple device see:

What does the Insights section show?

The Insights section gives you an overview of how your customers rate you, including a breakdown of your star ratings from 1 to 5, your average rating, total ratings, percentage of total reviews, and how many reviews you have responded to.

You will also see a sentiment analysis of your reviews, giving you a percentage split of positive and negative feedback.

  • Profanity or abusive language

  • Major inaccuracies

When you raise a dispute, you will be asked to provide verifiable proof to support your request, such as invoices or job records. Our team will review the dispute and take action where needed to keep feedback fair and useful.

Please note that reviews will not be removed simply because they are critical or unfavourable.

I already have a Stripe account can I use that one instead?

Yes, the hipages payment service is separate to your Stripe account. If you already have a Stripe account you can sign up to the hipages payments feature without having perform the KYC check again.

How soon can I request for payments after sign up is completed?

Stripe confirms verification via a Know-Your-Customer (KYC) check, which usually happens within seconds.

How do I add my bank account details?

How long does it take to do the KYC check?

If you have an account in Stripe already you wont need to do another KYC check, if you are new to Stripe it will take a few minutes. You’ll need identification so have that ready.

Can I turn payments feature off for some invoices?

Not at the moment, once its connected the link will be on all of your invoice emails.

Can I add the payment link to older invoices?

Once you have connected to hipages payments you can resend older invoices and the link will be included.

Using hipages payments

How does hipages make money from this?

Through the partnership with Stripe.

How will the payment appear on your customer’s bank account?

It will show as "on behalf of <tradie’s business name>" on the your bank account.

How will the payment appear in my bank account & how long will it take?

It will show as "hipages Payments Service" on your bank account. You will receive the funds within two business days.

If a customer refund is processed by the Support Team, when will they receive the refund?

Within 5-10 business days.

How will I know when I get paid?

You will see a hipages transaction in your bank account and the invoice status in hipages for business will automatically change to Paid.

Do I need a bank account connected to Stripe to receive payments?

For more information on setting up and managing payouts, see Stripe's payout support articles here: https://support.stripe.com/topics/payouts.

Account connections

Xero

What does the hipages for business integration with Xero do?

hipages integration lets you streamline your accounting by automatically syncing your invoices and customer information with Xero. In order to connect to Xero, you will require a Xero subscription. For information on how to set up a Xero account see, Sign up with Xero.

How do I connect to Xero?

To connect your Xero account to hipages for business, see Connecting to Xero.

How do I push my invoices to Xero?

Once your accounts are linked, any invoice that is sent to a customer will be submitted to Xero at the same time - no extra steps required. Some packages may have a limit to how many Invoices can be sent - each time the apps sync any new Invoices sent in the HP4B app will use up one of these. Please check your plan details with Xero.

How do I set or change the revenue account for Xero?

If you want to modify which Xero sales account your invoice items will be tracked against:

  1. On the To Do screen, select Profile → Settings & Support → Connected Apps.

  2. Under your Connected Accounts, select Xero.

  3. Select the revenue account currently being used to open a list of all your sales accounts in Xero.

  4. Select your preferred sales account.

Will my customers also be synchronised?

Yes. Here's how it works in each direction:

  • Xero to hipages for business: your Xero contacts sync into hipages for business, so you can start sending them quotes and invoices straight away.

  • hipages for business to Xero: only new customers who have been sent an invoice will sync across to Xero.

Note:

Customers who have only received a quote, or whose invoice is still in Draft, won't sync to Xero until an invoice has been sent.

How do I make use of inventory items in hipages for business?

Currently hipages for business won’t sync your inventory items in Xero. If this is an important feature for you please contact us at: hipages contact us page.

How are my tax rates handled?

hipages for business will make use of the default tax rates in Xero. Not that hipages for business is designed to work with organisation in Australia only. When you create an invoice you can select the tax rate that applies to each item and it will be synced with the equivalent default rate in Xero.

How is my currency handled?

hipages for business only supports Australian Dollar (AUD) as a currency as it's only available in Australia. If your Xero organisation isn't set up with AUD hipages for business will make this currency available in your organisation, but note that your organisation must be set up in Australia.

What if an invoice isn’t automatically submitted to Xero?

If you don’t see your invoice in Xero after having sent it to your customer, we suggest that you first disconnect and reconnect Xero, if there is a problem with the connection between the apps this usually resolves the issue.

Certain Xero packages have a limit to how many Invoices can be sent - each time the apps sync any new Invoices sent in the hipages for business app will use up one of these. If the Xero package has used all its monthly invoices hipages for business will not be able to push new invoices into Xero.

Can I disconnect my Xero account?

Yes, you can disconnect your Xero account from hipages for business. See Disconnecting from Xero to find out how you disconnect hipages for business from your Xero account.

How are my tax rates handled?

hipages for business will make use of the default tax rates in QuickBooks. When you create an invoice you can select the tax rate that applies to each item and it will be synced with the equivalent default rate in QuickBooks.
