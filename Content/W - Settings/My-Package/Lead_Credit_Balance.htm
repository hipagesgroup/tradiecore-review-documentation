Managing your package and credit usage

Effortlessly view and manage your lead credits, package upgrades, and view your usage history in the Credit balance section.

You can then view and manage your:

  • Check Available Credits: Monitor your remaining lead credits.

  • Add Credits: Purchase additional lead packs or upgrade your package for more opportunities.

  • View Package Details: Review your plan, benefits, and renewal date.

  • Upgrade your package

  • Track Accepted Leads: Review leads you’ve accepted along with their associated credit values.

Viewing your lead credit balance and package details

This section shows your current credit balance, your current package cost, and the package renewal date.

To view and track your hipages credit,

  • Select Settings → My Package → Credit Balance.

    The Usage screen is then displayed.

Adding credit or upgrading your package

To add more credit,

  1. Select + Add credits.

    The Upgrade screen is displayed.

  2. From here you can either:

    • Upgrade your package - Increases your monthly subscription cost and the number of lead credits you receive each month, based on the new package.

    • Top up your credits for further usage - Adds extra credits for the current month only. Your subscription cost and base credit allowance remain unchanged for the rest of your package period.

  3. Choose the upgrade or credit package that you want, then select Continue to payment.

    You can also upgrade your package from the Usage screen by selecting Upgrade Package.

  4. On the payments screen, choose a saved card or add a new card.

  5. If you selected a credit top-up, confirm your choice by selecting the No thanks, continue option when prompted about upgrading your package.

  6. Review and agree to the terms and conditions by selecting the confirmation checkbox..

  7. Select Complete purchase, to finish.

Checking your credit breakdown

The Credits Breakdown section of the Credit balance screen, shows you a detailed breakdown of,

  • Your monthly credits

  • If you have added a credit pack

  • Additional loyalty bonus credits

  • Expiry dates

Checking your usage history

The Usage history section shows a daily breakdown of how your lead credits are used. You can see how many credits were spent on each accepted lead, along with your remaining lead credit balance, helping you track usage and manage your spend more effectively.
