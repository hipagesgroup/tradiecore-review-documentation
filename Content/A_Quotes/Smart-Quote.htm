Creating and sending smart quotes

Smart Quotes makes it quicker and easier to create clear, detailed quotes by reducing the amount of manual typing required.

You can add job details in the way that suits you, by typing, using voice recognition, or attaching supporting files, so your quotes are accurate and easy for customers to understand.

Note:

Always review the quote and item list before sending it to your customer to ensure all details are correct.

If you can't see the Smart Quote feature, make sure you're using the latest version of the hipages for business app.

To generate a smart quote

  1. Open a job, and select Create Quote.

    The New Quote screen is displayed.

  2. Describe the work required, including the scope of work, materials, and any specific requirements. You can do this by:

    • Typing in the details, or

    • Selecting the microphone icon and speaking the job description. The app will convert your voice into text, which you can review and edit.

  3. For added clarity, select the add icon to upload photos or attach files, such as images or documents related to the job.

    Once complete, a quote is automatically generated using your price list history and current market data.

To review or edit the quote

  1. To make changes to the quote select Edit.

    The quote screen will then be opened.

  2. Make the changes that you want by either manually entering additional information or by changing the smart quote description. The Smart Quote description that you initially entered to generate the quote is shown at the top of the screen, which you can edit if needed.

    For more information on creating and editing quotes, see Creating a quote manually.

  3. If you are happy with the automatically generated quote, choose Send to send it to your customer. For more information see Emailing an invoice or quote to a customer.
